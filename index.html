We Protect Your Patents

With long-standing expertise in telecommunications patent licensing, we help patent holders protect their valuable patents from unauthorized use. By supporting patent monetization, we provide inventors with a new revenue stream and create incentives for R&D.

How Our Programs Work

1.

A patent owner (“client”) contacts Sol IP, LLC and provides information on its patent portfolio.

2.

Sol IP reviews the information provided and determines whether we will represent the client. Sol IP and the client negotiate and enter into a representation agreement.

3.

Sol IP contacts potential infringers of the patent portfolio and offers a license on behalf of the client. Sol IP handles license negotiations with the potential infringers.

4.

If necessary, Sol IP will initiate (or help the client initiate) legal actions against some potential infringers. The client will not be required to bear any financial burdens.
